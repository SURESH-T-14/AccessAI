## Issue 1: "User not found. Please sign up first"
**Meaning:** You're trying to login but email doesn't exist

**Solution:**
1. Click **"Continue with Email"** 
2. Click **"Sign Up"** tab (bottom)
3. Enter:
   - Full Name: Your Name
   - Email: your.email@gmail.com
   - Password: At least 6 characters
4. Click **"Sign Up"**
5. You'll get verification email
6. Click link in email
7. Now you can login!

---

## Issue 2: "Incorrect password"
**Meaning:** Email exists but password is wrong

**Solution:**
1. Click **"Continue with Email"**
2. Make sure on **"Sign In"** tab (not Sign Up)
3. Check password is correct
4. Check CAPS LOCK is off
5. Passwords are case-sensitive!
6. Try again

---

## Issue 3: "Email already in use"
**Meaning:** You tried to signup with email that already exists

**Solution:**
1. Click **"Continue with Email"**
2. Switch to **"Sign In"** tab
3. Enter same email + password
4. Login!

---

## Issue 5: No response / Blank screen
**Meaning:** Form not working, fields not accepting input

**Solution:**
1. Reload page: **F5**
2. Check all fields accept typing:
   - Full Name field
   - Email field
   - Password field
3. If still not working, clear browser cache:
   - **Ctrl+Shift+Del**
   - Check "All time"
   - Clear cache
   - Reload
